## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload PDF to S3">
    Drop your PDF into the designated S3 bucket to trigger automated processing
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automated Processing">
    PDFs are split, tagged with Adobe services, and enhanced with AI-generated alt-text
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accessibility Validation">
    Pre and post-remediation checks ensure WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download Results">
    Retrieve your remediated PDF or HTML package from the results folder
  </Step>
</Steps>
